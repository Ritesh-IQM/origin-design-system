# Rank Down Indicator

## Purpose
The **Rank Down Indicator** is used in the leaderboard to indicate that a user’s rank has **decreased** compared to the previous reference point.

It communicates negative movement clearly and immediately.

---

## Visual Description
- Downward-pointing triangular indicator
- Flat, minimal geometry
- Clear directional intent (downwards)
- No motion or animation

The shape is designed to be easily recognizable even at small sizes.

---

## Sizes
This icon is available in the following sizes:

| Size | Dimensions |
|-----|------------|
| Large | 32 × 32 |
| Medium Large | 28 × 28 |
| Medium | 24 × 24 |
| Small | 20 × 20 |

**Rules**
- Do not scale arbitrarily
- Use only the predefined sizes
- Maintain consistent proportions across all sizes

---

## Color
**Color Used - origin-action-error-default**


**Notes**
- The same color is used across all sizes
- Do not substitute with other colors
- Do not apply opacity, gradients, or overlays

---

## Stroke
- Uses the same stroke treatment as other leaderboard status indicators
- Stroke properties must remain consistent across all sizes

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Use When
- A user’s leaderboard position has decreased
- Displaying negative rank movement
- Showing downward change in comparative rankings

### Do Not Use When
- Rank has not changed
- Rank has increased
- Data is unavailable or loading
- Used outside leaderboard or ranking contexts

---

## Interaction
- Static icon
- Non-interactive
- No hover, focus, or animated states

---

## Accessibility
- Screen reader label:  
  **“Rank decreased”**
- Treated as informational, not actionable

---

## Category
- Icon Type: Status Indicator
- State: Negative
- Context: Leaderboard
